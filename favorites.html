My hobbies

One of the things I'm interested in is machinery. I find older machines such as typewriters very interesting and I think that it allows me to think more about how they work. I also have an old typewriter that I got from a garage sale that is pretty cool!

Another thing that I enjoy is video games. Not only do I enjoy them because they are fun to play, but I also like them because of the possibilities that they might have. I sometimes try to find certain modifications to the games that I enjoy in order to make them more interesting.
